Construction Law

Building projects require careful coordination of contracts, permits, and safety measures. My office advises contractors, owners, and suppliers throughout each phase.

Project Planning

Laying a Strong Foundation

Building a new structure begins with detailed contracts and accurate permits. Gabriel Smith prepares agreements that define responsibilities and timelines for every participant.

By addressing regulatory requirements early, we help avoid delays and keep your project moving forward.

Contract Disputes

Keeping Work on Track

Payment disagreements and claims of defective work can stall construction. We negotiate resolutions and pursue litigation when necessary to protect your investment.

Mechanic’s liens and bond claims are tools we use to secure the compensation you deserve under Alabama law.

Workplace Safety

Addressing On-Site Injuries

Accidents on a construction site raise issues of workers compensation and liability. We assist employers and contractors in responding to incidents and managing claims.
