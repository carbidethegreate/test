Construction Law

Building projects require careful coordination of contracts, permits, and safety measures. My office advises contractors, owners, and suppliers throughout each phase.

Construction law covers the drafting of agreements for new builds and renovations. Clear terms help avoid costly disputes over scope, payment, and performance. Alabama statutes and local regulations govern licensing and inspections, making compliance a critical step before work begins.

Disagreements over delays or workmanship can halt progress. I help clients enforce their contractual rights through negotiation or litigation, aiming to keep projects on track and within budget. Mechanic’s liens and bond claims also provide avenues for payment when conflicts arise.

Job site injuries lead to complex questions of liability. Understanding the interplay of workers’ compensation and general negligence law is essential when addressing claims brought by employees or third parties.
