Oil & Gas Law

Oil and gas operations involve intricate mineral rights and environmental oversight. We advise landowners and producers on the agreements and regulations that shape this industry.

Leasing and Ownership

Structuring Agreements for Exploration

Oil and gas leases define how mineral rights are developed. We draft and review these contracts to secure fair terms for landowners and producers.

Clear language protects property interests and outlines responsibilities during drilling and production.

Royalties and Accounting

Ensuring Accurate Payment Distribution

Production revenues must be properly allocated among interest holders. Our firm assists with royalty calculations and compliance with reporting standards.

We address disputes over deductions to help maintain strong working relationships among all parties.

Regulatory Oversight

Meeting Environmental and Safety Rules

State and federal agencies monitor drilling, pipeline construction and site remediation. We guide clients through permit requirements and inspections.
