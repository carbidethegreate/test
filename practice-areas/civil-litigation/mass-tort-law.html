Mass Tort Law

Mass tort cases arise when numerous people suffer harm from a single source, such as a defective drug or industrial accident. These matters involve intricate facts and significant legal coordination.

Though grouped together for efficiency, each plaintiff maintains an individual claim. Our firm investigates the unique circumstances of every case and works with experts to build a cohesive strategy that addresses liability and damages.

Courts often consolidate mass torts into multidistrict litigation or similar structures to streamline pretrial proceedings. We navigate these forums with precision, ensuring compliance with scheduling orders and preserving our clients' rights.
