Mass Tort Law

Large accidents and dangerous products can injure many people at once. From Opelika, Gabriel Smith coordinates cases so clients across Alabama can pursue justice together.

Commonly Shared Issues

Coordinating Complex Claims

When a single event harms many people, we investigate the source and gather evidence that links each injury.

By analyzing medical records and expert reports, we build a case that addresses both individual losses and the larger pattern of harm.

Managing Discovery

Gathering Comprehensive Evidence

Mass tort cases often involve thousands of documents. We organize this information and work with opposing counsel to keep the process moving.

Gabriel Smith monitors court deadlines so your claim remains on track, whether in federal multidistrict litigation or Alabama state court.

Negotiating Settlements

Achieving Collective Recovery

Resolution may come through global settlements that offer compensation to all affected clients.
