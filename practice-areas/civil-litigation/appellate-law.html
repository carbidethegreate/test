Appellate Law

Appellate courts review trial outcomes for mistakes of law. Gabriel Smith in Opelika prepares focused briefs that present your strongest arguments.

Analyzing Trial Records

Identifying Grounds for Appeal

Our review begins with transcripts and motions to find errors that may have influenced the verdict.

This careful approach ensures that we present the appellate court with a focused set of issues supported by the record.

Drafting Persuasive Briefs

Clear and Concise Arguments

Appellate briefs must explain how the trial court misapplied the law. Gabriel Smith drafts briefs that cite precedent while telling your side of the story.

Well-structured arguments help the appellate judges see why a reversal or modification is warranted.

Presenting Oral Argument

Addressing the Appellate Bench

During argument, we answer the panel's questions and highlight the key points from the brief.
