Civil Litigation

Civil disputes in Alabama require focused strategy from the start. Gabriel Smith guides clients from Opelika through lawsuits and negotiations across the state.

Early Case Evaluation

Understanding Your Legal Position

At the start of any claim we review contracts, correspondence and other records to pinpoint where the disagreement began. This insight helps determine if litigation is the proper path or if a negotiated solution serves you better.

Gabriel Smith relies on his experience in Alabama courts to gauge potential outcomes. By identifying the strengths and weaknesses of each side, he tailors advice to protect your interests.

Pretrial Procedures

Building a Solid Record

Once a suit is filed, gathering information becomes crucial. We draft pleadings, manage discovery and coordinate witness testimony so that the case develops in a complete and orderly fashion.

Our Opelika-based firm keeps strict timelines for filing motions and responding to opposing arguments. Every document is crafted with precision to ensure judges understand your position.

Trial and Resolution

Advocacy in Court

If settlement fails, we present evidence to the judge or jury with clarity. From opening statements to the final verdict, we work to communicate your story effectively.
