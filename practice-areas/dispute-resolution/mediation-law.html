Mediation

Mediation brings parties together with a neutral facilitator to identify common ground and craft durable solutions.

The Mediation Process

Encouraging Cooperative Dialogue

Mediation relies on a neutral facilitator who guides the discussion without imposing a decision. Parties retain control of the outcome.

This collaborative approach often leads to creative agreements that address each side's interests.

Confidential Negotiations

Addressing Issues in Private

Confidentiality allows participants to speak freely and explore solutions without fear of public exposure.

Resolutions reached during mediation can be incorporated into court orders to finalize the matter.
