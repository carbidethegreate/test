Nonprofit & Tax-Exempt Organizations

Charitable and community groups rely on tax-exempt status to support their missions. Our firm assists with formation, governance, and compliance.

Forming a Nonprofit

Establishing a solid foundation

Choosing the right entity type and drafting clear bylaws are vital to launch a successful organization. We file incorporation papers and secure state approvals.

Gabriel Smith uses his knowledge of Alabama law to guide founders through each step.

Obtaining Tax-Exempt Status

Navigating federal requirements

The IRS reviews detailed applications before granting recognition. We prepare Form 1023 and respond to questions about mission, governance, and finances.

Proper planning helps organizations start on the right track and avoid delays.

Maintaining Compliance

Protecting your nonprofit for the long term

Annual filings and record keeping are essential to retain tax-exempt privileges. We advise on board policies, charitable solicitations, and reporting obligations.
