Pro Bono Legal Services

Our commitment to public service includes providing legal help at no cost to those who qualify.

Community Commitment

Serving those in need

Gabriel Smith believes in equal access to justice. He offers pro bono representation to individuals who cannot afford counsel, focusing on civil rights and housing matters.

Each request is reviewed carefully to ensure the firm can provide effective assistance.

How Cases Are Selected

Assessing merit and available resources

We evaluate legal issues, potential impact, and community benefit. Cases with strong claims and clear need may receive priority.

When we cannot take a matter, we try to connect applicants with other local resources.
