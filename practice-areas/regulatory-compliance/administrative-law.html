Administrative Law

Administrative agencies regulate many aspects of daily life. Effective representation demands familiarity with agency rules and the procedural steps that guide each decision.

Licensing and Permits

Guidance for Businesses and Professionals

Many Alabama enterprises must secure permission from state or federal agencies before they may operate. Gabriel Smith advises clients on preparing thorough applications that meet each agency’s standards.

Careful planning can shorten the review process and reduce the risk of delays. From local permits in Opelika to statewide approvals, we help you move forward with confidence.

Agency Investigations

Responding to Official Inquiries

An unexpected letter or visit from regulators can disrupt your business. We guide you through document requests and interviews so that you remain compliant while protecting your rights.

Prompt involvement of counsel ensures that responses are accurate and complete. Gabriel Smith’s knowledge of administrative procedures helps you address concerns before they escalate.

Appeals and Judicial Review

Challenging Agency Decisions

When an agency issues an adverse ruling, you may appeal within the agency or seek relief in court. We assemble the record and file briefs that explain why the decision should be reconsidered.
