Cryptocurrency Recovery

Digital currencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum present unique challenges when funds are lost or stolen. Our firm guides clients through recovery strategies when crypto assets disappear due to fraud, hacking, or misplaced credentials.

Gabriel Smith leverages knowledge of blockchain technology and law enforcement coordination to help track transactions and pursue available legal remedies. Whether scammers emptied your wallet or an exchange failed to release funds, we analyze the situation and work to recover what was taken.

Cryptocurrency law is rapidly evolving. By combining technical insight with established asset recovery tools, we aim to restore clients’ holdings and deter further fraud.
