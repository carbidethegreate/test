Criminal Defense

Our firm defends clients accused of misdemeanors and felonies, protecting constitutional rights at every stage of the case.

Investigating Your Case

Building a Defense From Day One

Early action is vital when facing criminal allegations. I review police reports, interview witnesses, and examine forensic evidence to uncover every detail that can aid your defense.

By working closely with investigators and experts, I am prepared to challenge the state's narrative and identify weaknesses in the prosecution's case.

Navigating the Court Process

Knowledge of Alabama's System

The criminal justice system moves through several phases from arrest to trial. I guide clients through arraignment, discovery, and pre trial motions while protecting their constitutional rights.

Whether negotiating a plea or fighting charges in court, I provide clear counsel at every step so you can make informed decisions about your future.

Protecting Your Future

Focused on Long-Term Consequences

A conviction can limit employment prospects and jeopardize your freedom. My goal is to minimize penalties and pursue outcomes that allow you to move forward with your life.
