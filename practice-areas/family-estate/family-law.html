Family Law

Family relationships are at the heart of daily life. When those relationships face strain, you need a steady advocate to address divorce, custody, and support matters under Alabama law.

Divorce involves both emotional and financial complexity. From property division to determinations about spousal maintenance, I guide clients through each step with clear advice and measured counsel.

Custody decisions center on the well-being of the child. I seek practical solutions that honor parental rights while focusing on stability and the child’s best interests.
