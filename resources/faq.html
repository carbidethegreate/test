Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to common questions about our services and Alabama legal procedures.

What areas of law do you specialize in?

Gabriel handles personal injury, criminal defense, family law, and other civil matters. He is experienced with a broad range of cases throughout Alabama.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes. Your first meeting is free so we can review your situation and discuss how we may help.

How do your fees work?

Personal injury cases are usually handled on a contingency basis. Other matters may involve a flat or hourly fee depending on the work required. We will outline costs during your consultation.

Can you help with a case outside Opelika?

Gabriel serves clients across East Alabama and can take cases statewide. If your matter is outside his region, he can still assist or refer you to a trusted attorney.

How do I get started or contact you?

Call (334) 750-1729 , email gabrieljoseph.smith@gmail.com , or fill out our contact form. We will schedule an initial consultation to explain the next steps.
